What we offer

What is Mood Canvas?

Mood Canvas is essentially a mood journal that lets you record how you're feeling and why. With an integrated blog feature, you can not only document your emotions but also dive deeper into the context and triggers behind them. This comprehensive approach allows you to gain a holistic view of your mental and emotional landscape. By articulating your thoughts and experiences in the blog, you create a personal narrative that can be a powerful tool for self-reflection and self-discovery.

Keeping you safe

Mood Canvas is committed to ensuring the safety and privacy of its users. We employ state-of-the-art encryption and security measures to safeguard personal data and journal entries, ensuring they remain confidential and inaccessible to unauthorized individuals. Additionally, our platform encourages positive interactions and support within a community of like-minded individuals who share their mental health journeys.

About Us

Mood Canvas was created by 4 people who consider mental-wellbeing, self-care and emotional awareness to be paramount in today's fast-paced world. We understand that keeping track of our moods can provide valuable insights into our emotional patterns, helping us make better choices for our overall well-being.
